# DocumentDropzone

A drag-and-drop / browse file picker for identity-document capture during onboarding. Purely
presentational — the client-side glare/blur quality analysis and the server's document-type
verdict both live upstream (`useSubmitDocument` in `@clearline/data-access-onboarding`);
this component only reflects whatever `status` its caller passes and reports the selected `File`.

## User story

**US-CW-005 — Identity Document Verification & Compliance Screening** (part of EPIC-CW-002 —
Onboarding & KYB)

As a business owner or beneficial owner completing identity verification, I want clear, specific
feedback when my document photo fails quality or type checks, so I can correct the issue quickly.

## Acceptance criteria

- **AC-01.** A glare-affected capture shows "We couldn't read your document. Make sure it's
  well-lit, in focus, and fully in frame." with a "Retake photo" affordance.
- **AC-02.** A blurry capture shows "Hold the camera steady and try again."
- **AC-03.** An unrecognized document type shows "This doesn't look like a valid ID. Please upload
  a driver's license, passport, or state ID." with a "Choose a different file" affordance.
