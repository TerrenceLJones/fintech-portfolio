# GettingStartedRailEntry

The getting-started launcher's home in the sidebar rail — a pinned row at the foot of the nav list,
directly above the identity footer, with a slim ProgressBar and a monospace "X of N" count. Visually
distinct from the permanent nav destinations so it can retire on completion without the nav reflowing.

## User story

**US-CW-044 — Getting-Started Launchpad (Rail Entry, Panel & Progress)**

As a newly active member of an approved Clearline Organization, I want a "Getting started" entry
pinned in the sidebar rail with a slim progress bar, which opens a panel listing the getting-started
tasks my role can actually perform, so that I always know what to do next and how far along I am.

## Acceptance criteria

- AC-01 — A "Getting started" entry appears at the foot of the nav list, directly above the identity
  footer, showing a slim ProgressBar and an "X of N" count, visually distinct from nav destinations.
- AC-02 — Activating it (click, Enter, or Space) opens the launchpad panel.
- Accessibility (Design §17) — progress is a real `role="progressbar"` with the "X of N" figure always
  rendered as text alongside it, never conveyed by fill alone.
