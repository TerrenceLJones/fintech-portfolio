# UnsavedChangesFooter

The sticky Save / Discard bar that governs every form-bearing settings page (design §19 intro).
US-CW-034 establishes it as a shared organism the later settings form pages reuse. Purely
presentational: dirtiness tracking, the save, the revert, and the route-leave guard are the page's
job — this only surfaces the choice, pinned to the bottom of its scroll container, and renders
nothing when there are no changes.

## User story

**US-CW-034 — Personal Profile & Notification Preferences**

As any authenticated user, I want an unsaved-changes safety net on every edit — so I never lose work
and never save by accident.

## Acceptance criteria

- **AC-02 — unsaved-changes footer.** When I change any field without saving, a sticky
  "Save changes" / "Discard" bar appears and stays visible regardless of scroll; "Discard" reverts
  every field and dismisses the bar; navigating away with unsaved changes warns me first. This
  component renders the bar; the page owns dirty-tracking and the leave-guard.
